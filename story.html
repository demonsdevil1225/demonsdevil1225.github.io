🌧️ Rain & Gulab Jamun
⭐ chapter one · our rainy world
You and I, soaked in the rain, the chill making us shiver. No one else at home — just us, the silence, and the cold clinging to our skin. I held your hand, closed the door behind us, and lifted you just like that day at college. I spun you once, tasting the sweetness of your laugh, then carried you upstairs. In your bedroom, I laid you down gently, wrapping you in a tight, warm hug. My hands slipped under your t-shirt, rubbing your back, trying to stop your shivers. Then I peeled the wet fabric up just a little — only the softest hills visible — and I tasted you like gulab jamun, sweet and warm. I played with your stomach, felt your breath hitch. I pulled your t-shirt higher, up to your neck, and undid the clasp slowly, freeing the badham paal I love. My mouth found one, rough and hungry, while my other hand teased the other. I lost control — my fingers travelled down to the tofi, playing, slipping in and out like our secret game in the car. I turned you over, removed the last barriers, and licked the tofi from the top, tasting every layer — the chocolate, then the strawberry beneath it. For half an hour I worshipped you with my mouth, until your body heated under my touch. I asked if you wanted the milky bar inside. You didn't say no. I teased the tofi with it, then pushed in gently, pulling out, pushing again — slow at first, then harder. My mouth stayed on your badhams and gulab jamun, moving between them while I stayed deep inside you. After twenty minutes, I lifted you, and we became 69 — you tasting me while I devoured you, desperate and fast. I was close, but I wouldn't leave you until you shook and came undone. Only then, after you'd reached your high, did I let go. We hugged tight, breathless and tender, and fell asleep tangled in pain and pleasure. Four hours later I slipped out, leaving a kiss on your forehead. That's what I did — what we did — and it was ours. 🌙
✨ Dream & The Wedding
💫 chapter two · the dream that became real
In my dream, I was dancing with Muu — a duet in the rain, no one around. I lifted and spun her, threw her up like a baby and caught her, then shared imaginary gulab jamun. I felt like the luckiest man alive. 🌟 Then the alarm shattered it all. I woke with a jolt — the dream unfinished, my heart searching for the girl from that dream. I traced her voice, her face, but couldn't find her. A call came. A girl shouting: "Innum enna da thuukam, seekaram kealambi vaa!" I rushed to the bus stand, trying to trace the number, but couldn't. At the town hall, I saw her — cute eyes, the kind you fall into. My air pods played "Vinnalaam oli minnum alagai…" from Brahmastra, and everything clicked. She was the one. 💙 I'm bold, so I asked: "Can I join you in the bus?" She gave me a look — kevalam — but said "kk". I replied "Jolly, let's go!" We started talking formally, but it felt like we'd known each other forever. We shared everything — past, present, likes, fears — and fell for each other slowly, without naming it. Then one day she handed me a wedding card. My heart dropped. I took it with a fake smile, then later read the groom's name: it was mine. I hugged her in the middle of the street, not caring who saw. 💖 We got married. We even invited our exes — to show them what a successful love looks like. The wedding night? We were too tired, busy counting cash gifts. So we planned a honeymoon in France. 😄 The next day we flew to France. Mountains, bungee jumping, paragliding — we did it all. At night, under a full moon, we camped on a mountain top. With wine and a cold breeze, we crawled into the tent, held each other in an extra‑large hug, and slowly, with a dirty mood, we made that night unforgettable. 🌙✨ At the Eiffel Tower, we ate gulab jamun while looking over the entire city of Paris. The dream had come true — we had our whole world in each other. 🗼💕
👿 The Demon & The Hug

The demon entered the room and you hugged me tight — tighter than ever before. We fell into a deep, sweet kiss. Then you lifted me, just like you did at college, and threw me gently on the bed. You hovered over me, smiling between breaks, playing with my hair, my nose, my lips. 💜 Your finger traced from my throat down to the badham paal, teasing, remembering how I used to play with your throat. You found the tofi and explored inch by inch, staying at the top for five minutes, touching those sensitive spots I never told anyone about. 🌸 We moved together, slow and deliberate, lost in each other. 💫
